WHO WE ARE

Yuxi Global (that was called Yuxi Pacific at that time) was founded in Pennsylvania, U.S.A in 2005 by the entrepreneur Michael Puscar. In 2011, we opened a small office in Medellin, Colombia for the process of Research & Development and also by the quality of the local talent. Our sales and employee numbers began doubling each year since all our operations were moved to the city of Medellin.

In 2013, the company was acquired by the Blue Loop Capital and investment firm from the U.S.A that was owned by a single stakeholder, the serial entrepreneur, inventor and investor Kent Savage. In 2016, we rebranded ourselves as Yuxi Global, adopting a more modern image which reflects the maturity we have reached and our capacity of tackling challenges from all over the world.
